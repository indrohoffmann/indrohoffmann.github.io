Welcome to Christmas raffle


Christmas Raffle is for you if you want to raffle presents inside of your group... Is it family, friends, work mates or othe companions you have? Cristmas Raffle is here for helping you to create christmas raffle.

You choose the people you want to participate in the raffle, you choose your own draw conditions. As a result of the draw, all participants will find their person to give a gift to on this cristmas.

Simple! You don't have to start thinking about whom to give a gift on christmas eve. Do I have to do present for all my friends or colleges for Christmas? Or do I have to choose only best friends or worse do i have to prefer someone? Can anyone in the group can left without in a christmas rush? Or isn't it just reasonable that everyone gets gifts equally under the same conditions?

Yes! With Cristmas Raffle you don't have to worry about those things. You, just create raffle or particpate in it and you get your cristmas present reciever on your email.
